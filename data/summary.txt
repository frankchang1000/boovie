A giraffe finds a monkey friend who is extremely hungry. 
The giraffe uses her height to find food for the monkey.
The monkey reveals that other animals are struggling to find food.
The giraffe and monkey travel across the savannah to ask for advice from the lion. 
The lion suggests that all the animals move to the forest.
The animals, having heard of the giraffe's plan, are already gathered waiting for the giraffe.
